## ✅ Tone Checklist

Before posting, verify:

- [ ] No hyperbole ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "disrupting") — let the facts speak
- [ ] Short sentences. Hard stops. No rambling.
- [ ] Lead with the problem, land on the solution
- [ ] CTA is always present (pilot link or @mention)
- [ ] Avoids "AI chatbot" framing — KAI is a **Governance Guard**, not a chatbot
- [ ] Numbers and specifics preferred over vague claims (99% spam elimination, 14-day pilot, <15min SLA)
- [ ] If mentioning clients: Bancor · TrustSwap · Olas · Trac Network
